Today was a bit more relaxed than yesterdays schedule. We started around 9am to look at the valley of the rocks and then went to the black lagoon. For lunch we went back to the hostal and then loaded our backpacks on the jeep again to drive towards Uyuni. We made a stop a llama farm which was also the guide's hometown which is now abandoned.

We made one stop at a tourist town where they also make craft beer. This ‘town’ felt very fake although there were some local people living there. We tried some coca beer which tasted like any other beer. A group of small kids pushed a dog into my face which was very unexpected and then we left to drive the rest of the way to our hostel for the night.

Kaj climbed up an old train at the beer place
![Train]({{ '/assets/images/260329/train.jpeg' | relative_url }})

Our guide made a bonus stop to show us some giant cacti and a quinoa farm.

Rika with Quinoa
![Quinoa]({{ '/assets/images/260329/quinoa.jpeg' | relative_url }})

Our hostel was very interesting with built in stone beds. Since the weather was too cloudy, we decided to cancel the stargazing part of the tour and had some dinner and went directly to bed.
