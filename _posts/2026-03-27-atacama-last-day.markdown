Today was our last day in San Pedro. We got up at 4am to go on a tour to the El Tatio Geysers. We drove around 2 hours to get to the geysers which were at an elevation 4320m. We booked this tour last as we were told it’s a good way to acclimatise before the Uyuni tour.

El tatio
![Tatio]({{ '/assets/images/260327/tatio.jpeg' | relative_url }})

We reached the geysers right before the sunrise. It was very cold so the steam could be seen clearly. It made me want to go to the hot springs.

The geysers
![Geysers]({{ '/assets/images/260327/geysers.jpeg' | relative_url }})

After the geysers we went to a village called Machuca to eat breakfast. Then on our way back to San Pedro we visited a lagoon where we saw a herd of vicuñas and flamingos.

Vicuñas by the lagoon
![Lagoon]({{ '/assets/images/260327/vicuna.jpeg' | relative_url }})

Back at San Pedro we eat some empanadas and did the laundry to prepare for a very early start to Uyuni the following day. We enjoyed our time in the Atacama desert. The scenery was out of this world, and although I do not usually enjoy tours the ones we had were organised extremely well and it was a great experience.
